CampHawk Quick Cart

Auto-add to cart on Recreation.gov

⚠️ Read before enabling

This feature fills your dates and clicks Add to cart for you when you open a CampHawk alert link — running entirely in this browser, in your own signed-in Recreation.gov session. CampHawk never sees or stores your Recreation.gov login.

Automating Recreation.gov may violate its Terms of Service and can get your Recreation.gov account suspended or banned. You use this feature entirely at your own risk. CampHawk is not responsible for any action taken against your account.
